# KohaCon

KohaCon is a yearly get together of the Koha Community. Everyone is welcome and encouraged to attend. KohaCon is usually free to attend, and held (generally speaking) on a different continent every year.

All attendees are expected to abide by the [Koha Community Code of Conduct](http://koha-community.org/about/policy/code-of-conduct/).
