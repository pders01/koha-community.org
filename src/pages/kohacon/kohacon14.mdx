# KohaCon14
KohaCon is the international users’ conference for the Koha Open Source Library System; This year’s conference is hosted by [Universidad Nacional de Córdoba](http://www.unc.edu.ar) and will be held in the city of Córdoba.

- [Registration form](http://koha-community.org/kohacon/kohacon14/registration-2014/)
- [Call for presentations](http://koha-community.org/kohacon/kohacon14/call-for-papers/)
- [Schedule](http://koha-community.org/kohacon/kohacon14/schedule/)
- [Venue](http://koha-community.org/kohacon/kohacon14/venue/)
- All attendees are expected to abide by the [Koha Community Code of Conduct](http://koha-community.org/about/policy/code-of-conduct/).
