# Documentation

In addition to the following Koha Manuals, you can find helpful tutorials and guides on the [Koha Wiki](http://wiki.koha-community.org) or visit our section of [user created documentation](http://koha-community.org/documentation/other-docs/). 

To view the manual revision history, please visit our [git page](https://gitlab.com/koha-community/koha-manual/commits/master).

**Please keep in mind that these manuals are living documents and change on a regular basis and they can be open with PDF program as well such as [Soda PDF](https://www.sodapdf.com/) and others.**

## Installation instructions

The best instructions to use for most installations can be found on our wiki.

[Install using Debian packages on either Debian or Ubuntu](http://wiki.koha-community.org/wiki/Debian)
