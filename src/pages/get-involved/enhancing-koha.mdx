# Enhancing Koha 

Koha has development going on around the world, 24/7. Do you have a great idea you’d like to share, but don’t have a developer to make it happen? [Add your request to our bug database](http://bugs.koha-community.org) with a severity of Enhancement and a Change Sponsored value of “Seeking developer” or “Seeking cosponsors.”

**[See this page on our wiki for detailed instructions](http://wiki.koha-community.org/wiki/Category:RFCs)** and guidance for institutions or support vendors considering adding a feature to Koha.
