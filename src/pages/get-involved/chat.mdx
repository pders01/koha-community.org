# Chat

Come and chat to us, Kohaphiles of all types can be found on our real-time chat.

[<img src="https://koha-community.org/files/2022/09/element.png" />](https://app.element.io/#/room/#koha-community:matrix.org)

Connect using [Element](https://app.element.io/#/room/#koha-community:matrix.org) or pick another [client](https://matrix.to/#/#koha-community:matrix.org).

The channel is [logged](http://irc.koha-community.org/irclog/koha/).

You can also use IRC by pointing your favorite client at `irc.oftc.net:6667` and join `#koha` or [connect directly to the Koha channel through your browser](http://webchat.oftc.net/?channels=koha).
